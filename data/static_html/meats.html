Meat

For the poorest, meat is a rare luxury and is consumed once every two to three months. Those with a higher income eat meat more regularly and keep it in fridges and freezers at home. They have also access to meat in food markets and shops. The richest families usually buy meat packaged and processed by food manufacturers. Read more >>
