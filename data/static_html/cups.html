Cups

Cups are functional and hold liquids that we drink, but also have a social use. The poorest around the world have just a small selection for drinking both hot and cold drinks. Those with a bit more money have special ceramic cups for hot and glass or metal cups for cold drinks. The richest families have diverse shapes, colours and materials on display in their kitchen cupboards or shelves.
