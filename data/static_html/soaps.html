Soaps

Hand washing with soap reduces the risk of diarrhoeal disease, a leading cause of child mortality. The poorest families usually have a bar of soap to wash their hands with. Families with a higher income tend to buy soap in branded packages and keep it in a clean bucket or plastic box. The richest families buy liquid soap dispensers for hand washing, choosing from many fragrances and colours. Read more >>
