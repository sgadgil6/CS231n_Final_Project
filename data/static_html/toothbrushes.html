Toothbrushes

The poorest homes do not have toothbrushes, but use twigs and sand as toothpick tools instead. Families with a slightly higher income buy a plastic toothbrush to share since they cannot afford one each. The richest homes often buy special teeth cleaning products like flavored toothpaste, mouthwash or an electric toothbrush. Read more >>
