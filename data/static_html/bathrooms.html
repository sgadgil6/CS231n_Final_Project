Bathrooms

The poorest families, particularly in rural areas, practice open defecation or use shallow pit latrines. Families with a higher income, especially in cities, have more access to improved sanitation facilities. Many middle income homes use ventilated outhouses with deep pits, protecting food and water sources from contamination. The richest families use integrated squat or seating toilets with a flush or septic tank to safely separate human excreta from human contact. Read more >>
