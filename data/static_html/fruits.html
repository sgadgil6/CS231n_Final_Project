Fruits

Fruit consumption is important to prevent chronic disease. Low income families can afford to eat only locally grown fruit when in season. Those with more money can also choose their preferred fruit from markets and shops. The richest can eat fruit imported from all over the world and store it in special fruit bowls or in their fridge.
