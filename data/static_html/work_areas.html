Work areas

The poorest families work areas all look different and are often outside, since they often do physical work like rickshaw driving, sewing or farming. Those with a higher income sometimes install a desk to work or study at along with a comfortable chair. The richest homes have more disposable income to spend on work areas with desk lamps, computers and printing equipment.
