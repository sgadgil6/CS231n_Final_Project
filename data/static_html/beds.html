Beds

Materials, positioning and style of beds change across income levels. The poorest make do with natural fibres and unclean surfaces, while homes with more money sleep on raised platforms with purpose-made bedding for comfort. The richest homes often have more or less the same style mattresses, duvets and pillows, suggesting that there is globalized version of a comfortable nights sleep. Read more >>
