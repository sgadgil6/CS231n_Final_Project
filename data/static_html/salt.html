Salt

An essential part of every diet, most families store salt in their homes. In the poorest homes, families usually keep salt in brand-free plastic containers or pouches. Those with a higher income buy purpose made salt containers that have been packaged by food manufacturers. The richest families have a lot of different condiments to choose from and salt is placed in a designated space in the familys kitchen.
