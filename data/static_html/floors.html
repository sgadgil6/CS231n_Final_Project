Floors

Those with the lowest income, especially in rural areas, make do with dirt or sand flooring which is hard to keep clean. Those with a higher income tend to have a tiled or plastic lino floor that is cheap to install and easy to maintain. The richest homes invest in ceramic or stone tiles and wooden floorboards to suit their preference.
