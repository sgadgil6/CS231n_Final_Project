Medication

The variety and quality of medication that a family has available is dependent on income and the familys local healthcare system. For the poorest, medication is devastatingly expensive so they have just a small supply. Those with a higher income have a variety, but the amount they can afford is dependent on state subsidies. The richest families keep many painkillers, preventative medicines and vitamin pills in cupboards or on shelves.
