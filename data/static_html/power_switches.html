Power switches

Electricity that can be controlled by switch is convenient and safe for families. The poorest make do with lighting kerosene lamps for light or making a fire for heat. Those with a higher income have loose power fittings without switches, risking dangerous electric shocks. The richest families have light and power switches incorporated into the walls of their home.
