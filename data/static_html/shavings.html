Shaving

The poorest families use small blades or scissors to shave, while those with more disposable income buy purpose-made razors. Middle income families tend to buy the same style of razor everywhere in the world, usually temporary plastic ones.The richest families often have shaving foam, brushes and sometimes electric razors in their bathrooms.
