Vegetables

Vegetable consumption is important to prevent against chronic disease. Low income families eat locally grown produce, and usually just have a couple to choose from at any one time. Those with more money can choose their preferred type from markets and shops. The richest eat their favourite vegetables imported from all over the world and store them in their fridge or in purpose made vegetable containers.
